Welcome to Academics

Academics offers first-class education from the foundation stage to post-secondary levels. We are a British international school located in Lagos, with campuses in Anthony, Ikoyi, and Lekki. Our offers include Crèche, Preschool, Elementary School, Secondary School and Sixth Form

What We Offer

We prides ourself on the quality and breadth of its educational experience and the commitment of its remarkable mix of gifted and dedicated professionals.

Regular Classes

We are goal-oriented and strive for continuous improvement in teaching and learning. There is a conducive and productive learning environment that suits a range of abilities and learning styles.
